Anna is a New York based folk/contemporary christian artist whose music has been inspired by Bon Iver, Priscilla Ahn and Brooke Fraser, to name a few. She started singing when she was just 8 years old and learned how to play the piano and guitar as well. Her YouTube channel was first created in 2008 as a platform to share her music with others. She has written original songs as well as covered songs from other artists.

Over the years, she has been able to grow her passion for music at Church. One of her favorite bible verse is from Psalm 13:6,

"I will sing to the Lord, for he has been good to me."
Thank you for listening!
